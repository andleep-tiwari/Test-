MACT Judgment Template Generator

    NOTE

    This tool is a 'Drafting Assistance' within the meaning of Clause 7 of the Policy on the Use of Artificial Intelligence issued by the Hon'ble High Court of Gujarat as this tool generates a template which is a starting point and not a finished judgment. It must be read, reviewed, corrected where necessary, and supplemented by the Presiding Officer before it is pronounced.

    This tool computes the compensation figures in accordance with binding precedent of the Hon'ble Supreme Court of India. It does not apply judicial mind, record any finding of fact, determine negligence or liability, or draft the operative order. Every finding, every line of reasoning, and the final order is to be written by the Presiding Officer alone.
